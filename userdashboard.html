Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up VPN on my device?

Download the app for your platform from the Downloads page, install it, and log in with your credentials. The app will guide you through the setup process.

Can I use my subscription on multiple devices?

Yes! Depending on your plan, you can use SecureVPN on 5-10 devices simultaneously. Check your subscription details for your specific limit.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Manage Subscription, uncheck the "Auto-renew" option, and your subscription will not renew after the current period ends.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin and Ethereum for maximum privacy and convenience.
